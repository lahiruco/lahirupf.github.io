About Me

Hi, I'm Lahiru Shehan—a passionate UI/UX designer with over three years of experience crafting intuitive, modern interfaces for web and mobile platforms. My journey began with a love for design and a drive to make technology more human-centered, using tools like Figma to bring ideas to life.

Recently, I've been expanding my expertise into AI development with Python, exploring how intelligent systems can enhance digital experiences. Alongside my design and tech pursuits, I'm a full-time student of automotive technology, fascinated by the intersection of vehicles, design, and smart systems.

My goal? To blend creativity, technology, and my love for automobiles into innovative solutions—like SyncDrive, an app designed to make group road trips smarter and more connected.
